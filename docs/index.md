# Meshcore Scanner

iOS companion app for the [MeshCore](https://meshcore.co.uk) LoRa mesh network.
Pair your iPhone with a MeshCore BLE companion radio and map every repeater
you can reach from any location.

## Features

- Pairs over Bluetooth LE with a MeshCore companion device.
- Triggers discovery requests automatically as you move — by distance or by time.
- Logs each discovery with SNR, RSSI, GPS coordinates and timestamp.
- Draws scans, repeaters, GPS track and coverage shapes on a map.
- Organises scans into sessions.
- Exports all data to GeoJSON for analysis in QGIS, Mapbox, etc.

**All data stays on your device. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.**

## Requirements

- iPhone running iOS 26 or later
- A MeshCore-compatible BLE companion radio
